Example

Browser compatibility: Internet Explorer 10+, Edge, Chrome, Firefox and Safari.

  • Center and Center

    <div class="al-center">[Content]</div>

  • Top and Center

    <div class="al-top al-center">[Content]</div>

  • Bottom and Center

    <div class="al-bottom al-center">[Content]</div>

  • Right and Center

    <div class="al-right al-center">[Content]</div>

  • Left and Center

    <div class="al-left al-center">[Content]</div>
